Description
All-in-one stand and charging dock for joy-cons and pro-controller - This Quad Dock is designed to be both a storage stand and is also a fully functioning charging dock. (each Joycon can be charged either individually or simultaneously). Capable of Charging a Pro Controller and 2 Joy-Cons simultaneously (or without a Pro Controller on it, can charge upto 4 Joy-Cons) Smart charge indicator lights - This Joy-Con quad dock has individual LED charge indicator lights for each JoyCon slot which individually glow red when charging or glow green when fully charged. Do not disturb switch - In order not to disturb you at night, you can optionally turn the charge indicator lights off via a handy switch at the back. (Your joy-cons will still charge even when you do so). Smart charging supported - the smart charging capability will automatically detect and stop charging each one individually, to prevent them from overcharging when they are fully charged, which prolongs their battery life. USB Type C cable included - This Dock is powered via a USB to Type C cable (included in this pack) which allows you to plug it into literally anything with a standard USB socket, such as any of the USB sockets of the Nintendo Switch Dock - that came with your console or any USB to mains adaptor - (adaptor and console dock not included). Not included in this pack - Please note - the Joy-cons, pro controller, mains adaptor, Nintendo switch console, console dock (plus any other accessories shown in images) are mentioned and shown solely for usage demonstration purposes only and are not included with this pack. This pack contains - 1x dobe docking station plus 1x USB-to-type C charging cable.
